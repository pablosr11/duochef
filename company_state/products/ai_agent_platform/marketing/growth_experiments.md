# Growth Experiments for AI Agent Platform

## Goal: Drive 1k waitlist signups in 30 days, 10% conversion to beta users.

### 1. SEO Landing Page Optimization (Week 1)
- Keywords: 'no code AI agents', 'build AI agents without coding', 'AI workflow automation'
- Update landing_page.md with H1: "Build AI Agents Without Code - Join Beta Waitlist"
- Target: 500 organic visits/mo via Google.
- Test: A/B headline vs 'Autonomous AI Agents for Non-Tech Teams'

### 2. Referral Program (Week 2 Launch)
- Incentive: Refer 3 friends → Beta priority + free Pro month.
- Embed on waitlist form: 'Share with team'
- Channels: Social posts, email.
- Projected: 20% referral rate.

### 3. Twitter/X Thread Series (Ongoing)
- Post 1: Problem - Manual workflows suck.
- Post 2: Solution - Drag-drop AI agents.
- Post 3: Demo GIF + waitlist CTA.
- Hashtags: #NoCode #AIAgents #Automation
- Goal: 5k impressions, 200 clicks.

### 4. Partner with No-Code Influencers (Week 3)
- Reach out to @nocode, @bubble, @webflow communities.
- Offer: Exclusive beta + affiliate 20% rev share.
- Content: Guest posts, AMAs.

### 5. Content Flywheel: Use-Case Blog Posts
- 'Automate Gmail + Slack with AI Agents'
- Publish on Medium/HackerNews/Reddit r/nocode
- CTA: Waitlist link.

**Metrics to Track:** Waitlist signups, referral clicks, traffic sources (Google Analytics). Iterate weekly.
