# Interactive Engine Deployment

## Overview
This document outlines the deployment process for the interactive engine of the ChefAtHome application.

## Deployment Steps
1. **Ensure all functionalities are operational**: Verify that the interactive engine is fully functional, including the lesson flow, timers, and user checks.
2. **Testing**: Conduct internal testing to ensure the engine works as expected.
3. **User Feedback**: Gather feedback from initial users to identify any issues or areas for improvement.
4. **Final Adjustments**: Make necessary adjustments based on user feedback.
5. **Launch**: Prepare for the alpha launch by ensuring all features are ready and documented.

## Success Metrics
- User engagement rates
- Lesson completion rates
- Feedback from initial users

## Safety & Disclaimers
- Ensure users are aware of kitchen safety and food safety guidelines before starting lessons.
