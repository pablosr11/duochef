# Internal Testing Feedback Analysis

## Overview
This document outlines the analysis of feedback collected from internal testers regarding the ChefAtHome application. The focus is on identifying areas for improvement in user experience and product functionality.

## Key Findings
1. **User Experience**: Testers reported that the interactive lesson engine is intuitive but could benefit from clearer instructions at the beginning of each lesson.
2. **Content**: While the initial lessons are well-received, users expressed a desire for more variety in lesson topics and skills.
3. **Safety Features**: Testers appreciated the emphasis on kitchen safety but suggested including more visual aids and reminders throughout the lessons.
4. **Progress Tracking**: Users found the skill tree navigation helpful but requested more feedback on their progress and achievements.

## Recommendations
- Enhance instructional clarity at the start of lessons.
- Expand the lesson library to include a wider range of cooking techniques.
- Incorporate more visual safety reminders in lessons.
- Improve feedback mechanisms for user progress tracking.

## Conclusion
The feedback from internal testing provides valuable insights that can guide future iterations of the ChefAtHome application. Implementing these changes will enhance user experience and support our goal of helping users build confidence in their cooking skills.
