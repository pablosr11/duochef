# Bug Fix Preparation

## Objective
Prepare the Engineering team for potential bug fixes that may arise from testing the landing page and waitlist functionality.

## Steps to Prepare
1. **Set Up a Bug Tracking System**: Implement a system to log bugs reported by users or testers. This can be a simple spreadsheet or a dedicated bug tracking tool.
2. **Prioritize Bugs**: Establish criteria for prioritizing bugs based on severity and impact on user experience. High priority should be given to issues that affect core functionalities.
3. **Assign Responsibilities**: Designate team members to be responsible for addressing specific types of bugs, ensuring accountability and efficiency in the resolution process.
4. **Regular Review Meetings**: Schedule regular meetings to review logged bugs, discuss progress on fixes, and adjust priorities as needed.
5. **User Feedback Loop**: Create a mechanism for users to provide feedback on their experiences, which can help identify areas for improvement and potential bugs.
