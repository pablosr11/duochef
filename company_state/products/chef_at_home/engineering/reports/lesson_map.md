# ChefAtHome MVP Lesson Map

## Level 0 — Kitchen Orientation
- **Milestone**: Safe Setup
  - Lesson: Identify common kitchen tools (pot, pan, knife, cutting board).
  - Lesson: Stove/oven basics (heat settings, ventilation, basic fire safety).
  - Lesson: Importance of hand washing and cleaning surfaces.

## Level 1 — Heat & Water Basics
- **Milestone**: Boil Water Safely
  - Lesson: Filling a pot, using a lid, bringing water to a boil, and reducing to a simmer.
  - Lesson: Salting water (why and how much), understanding boiling vs. simmering.
  - Lesson: Safe draining techniques (using potholders, avoiding steam).

## Level 2 — Egg Fundamentals
- **Milestone**: Cook Eggs 3 Ways
  - Lesson: Scrambled eggs (low heat, consistent stirring).
  - Lesson: Fried eggs (oil control, achieving desired doneness).
  - Lesson: Boiled eggs (timing for doneness, basic peeling techniques).

## Level 3 — Pasta Essentials
- **Milestone**: Pasta with Simple Sauce
  - Lesson: Cooking pasta to al dente (timing, tasting for doneness).
  - Lesson: Reserving pasta water and its use in sauce emulsification.
  - Lesson: Basic garlic and olive oil sauce (avoiding burnt garlic).

## Level 4 — Rice & Roasted Vegetables
- **Milestone**: Reliable Staples
  - Lesson: Cooking rice (ratios, simmer, resting).
  - Lesson: Roasting vegetables (preheating oven, spacing, achieving browning).
  - Lesson: Introduction to the seasoning loop (salt, acid, fat, heat).

## Level 5 — One-Pan Meal Introduction
- **Milestone**: Multi-Step Confidence
  - Lesson: Basic sautéing and deglazing techniques.
  - Lesson: Sheet-pan meal workflow (prep, cook, serve).
  - Lesson: Timing two components (e.g., a protein substitute and vegetables).
