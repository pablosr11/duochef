# Implementation of Feedback Changes

## Changes Made Based on Internal Testing Feedback
1. **Enhanced Instructional Clarity**: 
   - Added introductory instructions at the beginning of each lesson to guide users on what to expect.

2. **Expanded Lesson Topics**: 
   - Introduced new lessons covering additional cooking techniques such as baking, grilling, and advanced knife skills.

3. **Visual Safety Reminders**: 
   - Incorporated visual aids and reminders throughout the lessons to emphasize kitchen safety practices.

4. **Improved Feedback Mechanisms**: 
   - Enhanced the skill tree navigation to provide users with more detailed feedback on their progress and achievements.

## Conclusion
These changes aim to enhance user experience and support our goal of building confidence in cooking skills. Further iterations will continue to refine these aspects based on user feedback.
