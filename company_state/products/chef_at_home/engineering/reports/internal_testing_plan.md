# Internal Testing Plan for ChefAtHome

## Objective
To ensure the functionality and usability of the ChefAtHome app through structured internal testing.

## Testing Phases
1. **Preparation**
   - Recruit internal testers.
   - Provide testers with access to the app via TestFlight.
   - Share testing guidelines and objectives.

2. **Testing Execution**
   - Testers will complete a full Level 0 flow, focusing on the interactive lesson engine.
   - Collect feedback on usability, bugs, and overall experience.

3. **Feedback Collection**
   - Use a feedback collection mechanism integrated within the app.
   - Schedule feedback sessions with testers to discuss their experiences.

4. **Analysis**
   - Analyze feedback and identify areas for improvement.
   - Prioritize fixes and enhancements based on tester input.

## Success Metrics
- Completion rate of the testing flow by internal testers.
- Number of bugs reported.
- Tester satisfaction ratings.
