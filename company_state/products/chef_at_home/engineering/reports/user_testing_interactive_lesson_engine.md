# User Testing for Interactive Lesson Engine

## Objective
Conduct user testing sessions to gather feedback on the interactive lesson engine, ensuring it meets user needs and expectations.

## Process
1. Recruit a diverse group of users, including complete beginners and busy adults.
2. Provide users with access to the interactive lesson engine.
3. Observe users as they navigate through the lessons, taking notes on their interactions and feedback.
4. Collect qualitative feedback through surveys and interviews after the testing sessions.
5. Document all feedback for further improvements.

## Success Metrics
- User satisfaction ratings
- Completion rates of lessons during testing
- Feedback on usability and clarity of instructions

## Next Steps
- Analyze feedback and implement necessary changes to improve the interactive lesson engine.
