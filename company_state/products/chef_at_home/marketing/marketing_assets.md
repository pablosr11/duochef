## Growth Experiment Ideas
1. **Social Media Cooking Challenges**: Encourage users to share their cooking progress on social media with a dedicated hashtag. Offer prizes for the most creative dishes.
2. **Referral Program**: Implement a referral system where users can invite friends to join the waitlist, earning rewards for each successful sign-up.
3. **Webinars with Cooking Experts**: Host free online cooking classes that showcase the app's features and provide valuable tips, driving traffic to the waitlist.
4. **Interactive Quizzes**: Create fun quizzes that help users discover their cooking style and recommend lessons based on their results, driving engagement.
