# Growth Experiment Plan: A/B Testing for Landing Page

## Objective:
To optimize the landing page for ChefAtHome to increase user engagement and conversion rates for waitlist sign-ups.

## Experiment Overview:
We will conduct A/B testing on two different versions of the landing page to determine which design and messaging resonate more with our target audience.

## Variants:
- **Version A**: Current landing page design and content.
- **Version B**: Modified landing page with a different headline, call-to-action button color, and layout.

## Key Metrics to Track:
1. Conversion Rate: Percentage of visitors who sign up for the waitlist.
2. Bounce Rate: Percentage of visitors leaving the page without taking action.
3. Time on Page: Average time spent on the landing page.
4. Click-through Rate (CTR): Rate of clicks on the call-to-action button.

## Duration:
The A/B test will run for two weeks to gather sufficient data on user interactions.

## Analysis:
At the end of the experiment, we will analyze the data to determine which version performed better and implement the winning design to improve overall user engagement.
