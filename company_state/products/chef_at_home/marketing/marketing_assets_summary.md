## 3. Social Media Posts
- **Content:** Engaging posts targeting complete beginners and busy adults to promote ChefAtHome and drive sign-ups for the waitlist.

## 4. Email Drafts
- **Content:** A compelling email inviting potential users to join the waitlist, highlighting the benefits of using ChefAtHome.

## Overall Strategy
- Focus on building engagement through multiple channels, including social media, email outreach, and an effective landing page. Aim to attract sign-ups and prepare for a successful alpha launch.
