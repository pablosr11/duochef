# Welcome to ChefAtHome

## Discover the Joy of Cooking
Are you tired of takeout and looking to learn how to cook? ChefAtHome is designed for complete beginners who want to build their cooking skills in a safe and engaging way. Just like Duolingo teaches languages, we teach you practical cooking skills through short, interactive lessons that fit into your busy schedule.

### Why Choose ChefAtHome?
- **Interactive Lessons**: Our app features bite-sized lessons that guide you step-by-step through essential cooking techniques.
- **Skill Progression**: Start from the basics and unlock new levels as you master each skill. From boiling water to preparing multi-step meals, we help you gain confidence in the kitchen.
- **Safety First**: Learn kitchen safety and food handling in a fun and engaging way, ensuring you can cook safely.
- **Gamified Experience**: Track your progress with XP, streaks, and milestones that keep you motivated and coming back for more.
