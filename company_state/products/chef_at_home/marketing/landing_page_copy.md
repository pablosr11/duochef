Welcome to ChefAtHome, where cooking becomes as easy as learning a new language! Our app is designed to help you master practical cooking skills from the comfort of your home.

- **Skill Progression**: Start with the basics like boiling water and progress to delicious multi-step meals. Unlock skills at your own pace!
- **Safety First**: We prioritize kitchen and food safety, ensuring you learn to cook confidently and safely.
- **Gamified Experience**: Track your progress with our skill tree navigation and enjoy the satisfaction of small wins as you complete each lesson.
