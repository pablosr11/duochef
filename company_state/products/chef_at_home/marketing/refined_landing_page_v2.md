# ChefAtHome: Your Kitchen Confidence Starts Here

## Learn to Cook in 5-Minute Bursts, Just Like Learning a Language!

Are you tired of takeout and intimidated by recipes? ChefAtHome is your fun, gamified guide to mastering essential cooking skills, one bite-sized lesson at a time. Whether you're a complete beginner or just want to cook more confidently, we'll take you from boiling water to weeknight dinners with daily practice and clear progress.

### Why ChefAtHome?

*   **Learn at Your Pace**: Short, engaging lessons fit into your busy schedule (5-10 minutes a day).
*   **Build Real Skills**: We focus on fundamental techniques – boiling, sautéing, roasting – not just recipes.
*   **Cook Safely**: Kitchen safety and food safety are built into every step.
*   **See Your Progress**: Track your skills with XP, streaks, and satisfying milestone badges.
*   **Gain Confidence**: Friendly guidance and error recovery mean you'll always know what to do.

### From Zero to Delicious

Start with the absolute basics like boiling water and handling knives, and progress through mastering eggs, pasta, rice, and vegetables. Our skill tree will guide you to confidently prepare multi-step meals and impress yourself (and others!).
