## Growth Experiment Ideas for ChefAtHome

1. **Referral Program:** Encourage users to refer friends to join the waitlist by offering incentives (e.g., exclusive cooking tips or early access to app features).
2. **Social Media Challenges:** Create a cooking challenge on platforms like Instagram where users share their cooking attempts using a specific hashtag. Offer prizes for the best entries.
3. **Partnership with Cooking Influencers:** Collaborate with cooking influencers to promote the app and share their experiences with their followers.
4. **Email Drip Campaign:** Send a series of engaging emails to waitlist sign-ups with cooking tips, sneak peeks of the app, and testimonials from early testers.
5. **Content Marketing:** Publish blog posts on cooking tips, beginner recipes, and kitchen safety to attract organic traffic and drive sign-ups.
