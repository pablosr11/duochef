# ChefAtHome Social Media Content Calendar (First 4 Weeks Post-Launch)

**Goal:** Drive app downloads, build community, and highlight key features.

**Target Audience:** Beginner cooks, busy adults, those intimidated by cooking.

**Platforms:** Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, potentially Pinterest.

**Frequency:** Daily posts across platforms, with a mix of content types.

---

### Week 1: Launch & Introduction

*   **Theme:** "Meet ChefAtHome: Your New Culinary Coach!"
*   **Content Focus:** Announce launch, introduce the "Duolingo for cooking" concept, highlight ease of use and safety.

    *   **Day 1 (Launch Day):**
        *   **IG/FB Post:** "We're LIVE! 🎉 Learn to cook with ChefAtHome - the fun, gamified app that makes cooking easy. Download now! [Link in Bio] #ChefAtHome #LearnToCook #CookingApp #NewApp"
        *   **TikTok:** Short, energetic video showcasing the app's gamified interface (streaks, XP) with a voiceover: "Tired of takeout? Learn to cook in 5 mins a day! #CookingHacks #EasyRecipes #ChefAtHomeApp"
    *   **Day 2:**
        *   **IG Story:** Poll: "What's your biggest cooking fear?" (e.g., burning food, undercooking, complex recipes).
        *   **FB Post:** "From boiling water to weeknight dinners – ChefAtHome guides you every step. See our lesson map! [Link to Landing Page/Website]"
    *   **Day 3:**
        *   **IG Reel:** "Kitchen Safety 101 with ChefAtHome" - quick tips on knife handling and stove safety, referencing the app's safety lessons.
    *   **Day 4:**
        *   **TikTok:** "Can you cook THIS?" challenge - users try a simple task (e.g., perfectly boiled egg) and tag ChefAtHome. Show app's step-by-step guide.
    *   **Day 5:**
        *   **IG Carousel:** "5 Reasons You'll Love ChefAtHome" (Gamified learning, bite-sized lessons, safety focus, skill-building, confidence boost).
    *   **Day 6:**
        *   **FB Post:** "Master the perfect scrambled egg! 🍳 Our app breaks it down into simple steps. Try it today! [App Store Link]"
    *   **Day 7:**
        *   **IG/FB Post:** "Congrats on your first week with ChefAtHome! Keep that streak going!" - encourage daily engagement.
