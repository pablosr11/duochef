# Welcome to ChefAtHome!

## Your Journey to Culinary Confidence Starts Here!

Are you tired of feeling intimidated by recipes? Do you wish you could cook with confidence, even if you only have a few minutes to spare? ChefAtHome is designed for you! 

### Why Choose ChefAtHome?
- **Interactive Lessons**: Just like Duolingo for languages, our app offers short, engaging lessons that fit into your busy lifestyle.
- **Skill-Building Focus**: We teach essential cooking techniques first, ensuring you're ready to tackle any recipe.
- **Gamified Progress Tracking**: Unlock levels and milestones as you improve your cooking skills, keeping you motivated!
- **Safety First**: Kitchen safety and food safety are prioritized to ensure a secure cooking environment.

### What You Will Learn:
- Basic techniques like boiling water and knife skills.
- Intermediate skills such as sautéing vegetables and cooking rice.
- Advanced dishes and multi-step meals as you progress.
