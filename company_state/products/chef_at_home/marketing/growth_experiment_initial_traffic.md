# Growth Experiment: Initial Traffic Generation for ChefAtHome

## Objective
Drive initial traffic to the ChefAtHome landing page and encourage early sign-ups/downloads.

## Hypothesis
Targeted social media ads and content marketing focusing on the "Duolingo for cooking" angle and beginner-friendly approach will attract users who are interested in learning to cook but feel intimidated by traditional methods.

## Experiment Design

**1. Social Media Ad Campaign:**
*   **Platforms**: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok
*   **Targeting**: Users interested in cooking, food, language learning, self-improvement, busy professionals, students.
*   **Ad Creative**: Short video ads (15-30 seconds) showcasing the app's gamified interface, quick lessons, and the "boiling water to weeknight dinner" progression. Use the ad hooks developed for the landing page.
*   **Ad Copy**: Emphasize the 5-minute daily learning, the "Duolingo for cooking" analogy, and the confidence-building aspect. Include a clear Call to Action (CTA) to "Learn More" or "Download Now" linking to the landing page.
*   **Budget**: Allocate a small initial budget for testing.
*   **Metrics**: Click-Through Rate (CTR), Cost Per Click (CPC), Landing Page Views, Conversion Rate (sign-ups/downloads).

**2. Content Marketing / Influencer Outreach:**
*   **Content**: Create blog posts or social media content around topics like "5 Easy Cooking Skills for Beginners," "How to Overcome Recipe Intimidation," or "Learn to Cook in 5 Minutes a Day." Integrate ChefAtHome as the solution.
*   **Influencer Outreach**: Identify micro-influencers in the food, lifestyle, or education space who resonate with the target audience. Offer them early access or a partnership to promote the app.
*   **Metrics**: Reach, Engagement, Website Traffic from content, Referral Downloads.

**3. App Store Featuring (if possible):**
*   Prepare a strong pitch for app store editors, highlighting the unique "Duolingo for cooking" concept, the focus on safety, and the beginner-friendly approach.

## Timeline
*   **Week 1-2**: Set up ad campaigns, create initial content, begin influencer outreach.
*   **Week 3-4**: Monitor campaign performance, optimize ads, scale successful content, and follow up with influencers.

## Success Metrics
*   **Primary**: Number of app downloads and active users (Day 7 retention).
*   **Secondary**: Landing page conversion rate, social media engagement, cost per acquisition (CPA).
