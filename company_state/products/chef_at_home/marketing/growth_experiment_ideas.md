### Growth Experiment Ideas

1. **Referral Program**: Encourage users to refer friends by offering rewards (e.g., exclusive recipes, early access) for each successful sign-up.
2. **Social Media Challenge**: Create a cooking challenge where users share their cooking attempts using a specific hashtag. Offer prizes for the most creative posts.
3. **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate with cooking influencers to promote ChefAtHome through live cooking sessions or tutorials, driving traffic to the landing page.
