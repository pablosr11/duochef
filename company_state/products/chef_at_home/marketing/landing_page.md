# ChefAtHome: Your Cooking Journey Starts Here!

## Welcome to ChefAtHome!
Are you ready to embark on a culinary adventure? ChefAtHome is designed to help you learn cooking skills in a fun, engaging, and safe environment. Just like Duolingo revolutionized language learning, we are here to revolutionize your cooking experience.

### Why ChefAtHome?
- **Interactive Lessons**: Our lessons are short and designed for busy adults who can spare just 5-10 minutes a day. 
- **Gamified Experience**: Unlock milestones and track your progress as you become more confident in the kitchen.
- **Safety First**: We prioritize kitchen and food safety, ensuring that you learn to cook safely and confidently.

### Who is ChefAtHome For?
- **Complete Beginners**: If you’ve never cooked before and feel intimidated by recipes, we’ve got you covered!
- **Busy Adults**: Perfect for those who have limited time but want to learn practical cooking skills.
- **Skill-Building Progression**: Our structured approach helps you build your skills gradually, from boiling water to mastering multi-step meals.
